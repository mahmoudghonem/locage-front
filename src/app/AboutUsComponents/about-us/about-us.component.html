History
  • Locage, Egypt's no. 1 online mall was established in July 2012 with the aim and vision to become the one-stop shop in Egypt with implementation of best practices both online and offline.
  • Locage is the largest online mall store in Egypt.
  • At inception we did an average delivery time of a week, today we do, on average, delivery in 1-5 days.
  • Initially starting with 3 employees, Locage presently has a staff strength of 350 young and entrepreneurial Egyptians.
  • Deliveries to all cities in Egypt.
